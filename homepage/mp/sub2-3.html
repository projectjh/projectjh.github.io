PETASO

[PETASO] The technology and device of real-time cloud transmission of drone high-resolution photos and images

Through our service platform, we implement the drone environment where the files of high-resolution photos and images shot by drones and meta information are transmitted in real time. A drone, which has PETASO (the technology and device of replacing micro SD cards of all commercial drones) built in, makes it possible to analyze large data, to process 3D modelling automatically, to make experts’ reviews and to make a decision very fast.

  • Transmitting files in real time while a drone takes photos is a key to the advancement of the SID platform service for implementing automated workflows, such as real-time analysis & decision-making and fast sharing of image data.
  • Application to the analysis, inspection, and measurement of large facilities, including solar/wind power plants, transmission towers, construction sites, and bridges.
  • Fast data uploading and sharing is a key to successful drone application business in terms of automated workflows including photographing with drones, data analysis & inspection, and decision-making.

PETASO Architecture

PETASO makes it possible to obtain drone image data in real time in the inspection & measurement areas of large facilities, including solar/wind power plants, transmission towers, construction sites, and bridges, and to implement seamless business workflow between a site and a remote platform.

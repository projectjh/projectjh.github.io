WOOD WASTE RECYCLING

Dongsuh Industry is equipped with various mobile vehicles and devices for logging, clearing & gruffing, small-size transportation, collection and chipping

Small-size transportation and collection in the sites of logging and clearing & gruffing

  • - Construction field, logging, and clearing & gruffing
  • - A site with wood wastes
  • - Small-size collection in a site

Insertion of chipper

  • - Classification of soil and wastes
  • - Top branches, Hard wood, Roots
  • - Wood with pine wilt disease

Crushing, Chipping

  • - Chipping with less than 100mm of screen
  • - Chipping with less than 500mm of screen

Recycling

  • - Compost for cattle sheds, orchards, and ornamental trees
  • - Raw material of plywood, compost factory
  • - Use as a cover for golf course and a variety of fruit farms
  • - Use as compost material for livestock farm and organic fertilizer factory
  •   Logging : Shortened construction period with professional loggers
  •   Clearing & gruffing : Equipment technicians with more than 15 years of career
  •   Wood chipping and recycling treatment by the development of gold courses and resorts : specialized sawdust production line
  •   Chipping treatment of wood infected with pine wilt disease (fumigated wood) : the nation's first company of chipping and recycling wood with pine wilt disease

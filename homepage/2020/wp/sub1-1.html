Greetings

WatchPoint, a business partner
that regards the improvement of customer value as the first priority

WatchPoint is an ICT development company that develops the B2B solution & service based on big data, blockchain, and security technology.

As a solution company of experts with various experiences in security and energy, WatchPoint aims for the creation of customer value.
The major business areas are the information leak control system, SSO(Single Sign On), and entry-level EMS, by developing and supplying the excellent solutions that could increase the customer value.

The information leak control system is providing the efficient system suitable for customers’ environment based on various establishment experiences for many years.
The SSO(Single Sign On) is contributing to the improvement of work efficiency of executives and staff members, through the SSO interlocking module that is safe and easy to apply.
The entry-level EMS solution is providing the actual reduction of energy cost and control function of relevant facilities to small/medium-sized companies or small-scale customers.

WatchPoint will become a business partner that considers the improvement of customer value as the first priority, based on the accumulated knowledge and technical knowledge of whole executives and staff members.

SOLVIT

Support OS Window10 RAM More than 4GB
CPU More than Intel i5 HDD More than 100MB
Resolution Full HD

With the use of our drone platform, we supply the service for efficiently collecting, analyzing, and managing the defect data of solar power plants.
SOLVIT Platform consists of auto drone flight, infrared thermal images, AI based analysis service, and the cloud service for managing drone data.

Service Concept

Main features

Fast & accurate PV module inspection and analysis

Infrared thermal images shot automatically in five minutes per 1MW

Stable infrared thermal images

Precise aerial photograph based precise auto flight path setting

The function of displaying the location of a bad cell with a marker

Pixel-by-pixel temperature information check and failure check

Stable infrared thermal images

Precise aerial photograph based precise auto flight path setting

Insight of power plant defects

Finding overall defects of power plant at a look

Offering of hierarchical defect map of power plant

Defect tracking with Module/String/Array information

Application effect

SOLVIT Platform rightly and accurately solves the solar PV module failure, which is hard to be identified with the naked eye.

  • When a power plant is completed

    Easy review of a site and increased design completion with the use of a drone

  • PV panel inspection

    Inspection of defective modules and replacement with new modules

  • Efficient maintenance

    After-sales-service for defective modules (right before the end of three years)
    Inspection of all failures (annually regular inspection)

  • Sales increase

    Profit increase through the improvement in power generation efficiency
